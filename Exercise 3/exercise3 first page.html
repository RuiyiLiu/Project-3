Share your daily work to help with your procrastination
Time is a very important part of life. Most teenagers now have procrastination. This is not a disease, but a living habit. But procrastination plagues many people who don't want to delay anything. They don't know how to change their long-term habits. This website is to help people with these difficulties to share their work. At the same time, they don't want others to see personal privacy. The website will only help you share your working hours and let more people supervise you.
